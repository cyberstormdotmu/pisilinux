Before Posting¶

Please first try to find help documents or look into list archives about your problem. If you do not find a suitable answer, you can write to a relevant list.

Posting¶

  1. Mail lists generally has a high traffic, thousands of copies are posted to many machines, so please:
    1. keep mail short
    2. do not add attachments
    3. do not cross post
  2. Do not use HTML messages, because HTML messages:
    1. Can contain malicious contents
    2. Wastes bandwith
    3. Does not always work
    4. Renders slowly
    5. Are not suitable for digested lists

  3. If you start a new topic, please create a new message, do not try to reply to an existed message.

  4. Do not recycle messages. Recycling messages is replying to an existing thread by changing the thread name. This creates confusion and diverts the number of people replying to the topic.

  5. Give good subject line. That helps you get help from the right people quickly.

  6. Use bottom or inline posting and trim irrelevant parts. Your message will be more organized and readable.

  7. Be polite in your messages. Never sevear or be rude to anyone.

  8. If one of the mails is offended you personally, please send a private message about your displeasure, instead of sending it to the mailing list.

  9. No gender or nationality is abuse is allowed on the lists.

  10. Be careful when using sarcasm and humour. Without face to face communications your joke may be viewed as criticism. When being humourous, use emoticons (smileys) to express humour.

  11. Do not send invitations or social networking connections through the mailing list.

  12. Avoid commercial messages.

  13. Avoid long signatures and disclaimers.

  14. Do not use CAPITALS, this means shouting, instead you can use _something_.

  15. Do not send same message many times. The other members of the list generally volunteers and can reply to your message in 24-48 hours.
